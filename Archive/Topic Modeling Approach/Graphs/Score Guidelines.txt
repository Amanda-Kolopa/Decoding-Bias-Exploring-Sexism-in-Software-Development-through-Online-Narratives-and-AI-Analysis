Calinski-Harabas: the higher the score, the better the clustering performance

Davies-Bouldin: the lower the score, the better the clustering performance

Silhouette Method: A score of 1 means each data point is unlikely to be assigned to another cluster, while a score close to 0 means each data point could be easily assigned to another cluster. A score close to -1 means the datapoint is misclassified

SSE Elbow: look for bend in the graph (i.e., elbow)
